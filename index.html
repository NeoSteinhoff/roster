Roster is built around memory, timing, and prep.

FAQ

The basics people need before they install.

Is Roster for friends or work contacts?

Both. It is built for any relationship where memory, timing, and personal context matter more than a sales pipeline.

Can I import from another system?

Yes. CSV import, duplicate cleanup, merge flows, archive, and readable export are all part of the switching path.

Does it work offline?

Yes. Roster is local-first now, with cloud backup and sync as the next major paid feature layer.
